Simulating the Effects of Door-In-Door-Out Interventions
Khaled Abughoush*, Zahra Paranianour, Bruce Ankenman, Jane Hall, Ohad Perry, Shyam Prabhakaran
Stroke, 2024
project page / AHA Journals

A simulation study of stroke care transfer processes found that implementing new protocols, particularly direct CT/CTA scanning and improved paramedic handoffs, could reduce door-in-door-out times by 33 minutes (19%) at primary stroke centers transferring patients to comprehensive stroke centers.
