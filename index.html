Unveiling the Dynamics of Open-Source AI Models: Development Trends, Industry Applications, and Challenges
Mana Azarm, Esmat Sangari, Khaled Abughoush
HICSS, 2025
arXiv

Open-source AI model development has seen exponential growth since 2020, with text processing dominating the field while audio and image processing lag behind, and just 1% of models, primarily in text processing, account for the majority of user engagement

Simulating the Effects of Door-In-Door-Out Interventions
Khaled Abughoush*, Zahra Paranianour, Bruce Ankenman, Jane Hall, Ohad Perry, Shyam Prabhakaran
arXiv, 2024
project page / arXiv

A simulation study of stroke care transfer processes found that implementing new protocols, particularly direct CT/CTA scanning and improved paramedic handoffs, could reduce door-in-door-out times by 33 minutes (19%) at primary stroke centers transferring patients to comprehensive stroke centers.
